About Us

A fresh take on an old art, we at Tough Tiger Tattoo are happy to welcome all patrons for all styles. We offer an extensive array of tattoo designs from a crew of talented, and positive artist. Working with people to find a piece they will love forever, as well as an inviting environment. We believe tattoos are an art and we pride ourselves on providing a memorable experience and a comfortable space that is cozy to all. Everyone is welcome and encouraged to check us out.
